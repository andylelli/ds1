These guides are optimized for Apple's built-in Spoken Content feature.

  1. Enable the feature: Go to Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content and toggle on Speak Screen.
  2. Open a guide: Tap a card above to open the document.
  3. Listen: Swipe down with two fingers from the very top of the screen to hear the content.
  4. Tip: Use the on-screen controller to adjust speed (1.0x or 1.5x recommended).
