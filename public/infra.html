Component Configuration Mode Description
Shop Platform
Controls where products are created and orders are fetched from.
Advertising Platform
Controls where ad campaigns are launched and tracked.
Social Media
Controls where social posts are published.
Market Research
Controls data sources for product research (Google Trends, etc).
Supplier Network
Controls product sourcing and fulfillment.
